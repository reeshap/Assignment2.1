Scrambled Eggst

Eggs are one of my favourite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a frying pan.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Ones butter has melted add the cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the egg from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as is you are making an omlette>.
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served).>
